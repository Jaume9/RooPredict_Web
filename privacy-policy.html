Privacy Policy for RooPredict

Last updated: September 2025

1. Information We Collect

RooPredict does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data entered by users (roulette numbers, spin history, and analysis results) is stored locally on your device and is not shared with third parties.

2. Local Data Storage

  • Roulette spin history is stored locally on your device using secure local storage
  • Statistical analysis data is processed and stored locally
  • No data is transmitted to external servers or cloud services
  • All data can be cleared using the "Clear All Numbers" function within the app
  • Data remains on your device unless you explicitly delete it

3. Data Processing

RooPredict processes data locally for the following purposes:

  • Analyzing dealer patterns and spin sequences
  • Calculating statistical probabilities
  • Generating prediction recommendations
  • Displaying historical data and trends

All processing occurs on your device and no data leaves your device.

4. Third-Party Services

This app does not use third-party analytics, tracking services, or advertising networks. RooPredict operates completely offline and does not integrate with any external data collection services.

5. Device Permissions

RooPredict does not require any special device permissions such as:

  • Camera access
  • Location services
  • Contact access
  • Microphone access
  • Internet connectivity

6. Children's Privacy

RooPredict is intended for users 18 years and older due to gambling-related content. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 18. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features
  • We recommend using device passcodes or biometric authentication
  • Regular device updates help maintain security

8. Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app
  • Modify: You can edit or delete individual entries
  • Delete: Use the "Clear All Numbers" function to remove all data
  • Portability: Data is stored locally and can be accessed directly

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Notifying users through app updates when significant changes occur
